Llama


My first cat is named Llama.

Birthday: February 10th, 2016

Likes: food, sleep, Celeste.

Sentience: yet to be gained.

Breed: Siamese.

Wisdom: -5

Alignment: Lawful evil

Athletics: nuh uh

She's very very silly and has many enemies in this world. One of them is my brother, who feeds her. She's very impatient and very vocal about getting fed, and my brother often tells her to quit yapping. I don't think this is very likely to happen.

We didn't really know about adoption or anything when we got her. So, the first place we looked for a cat was Kijiji. Shout out to mike, who sold her to us. He's a real one. When we went to go meet her, we also got to meet her brother and sister. They were completely indistinuguishable from her so like I don't know if the cat we met is really the one we got. But that's okay!! They were all really cool. We tried to meet Llama's parents but they kept hiding.

She is simultaneously both my clingiest cat and most aggressive cat. Sometimes I wish she was less evil but unfortunately her prefrontal cortex has probably developed and there is a very low chance of her personality (catsonality?) changing.
