Octopuss

My second cat is named Octopuss.

Birthday: March 14, 2018

Likes: going outside, my mom, water (he is constantly at the water fountain. Like. Everytime i see him. I'm so proud of him.)

Intelligence: it depends. RNG-based

Alignment: Chaotic good

Current hit points: 999999999

Constitution: +9999999 (I have seen him drink from puddles like a billion times. How did he survive that)

This guy has no enemies except for Llama. He's very sweet and every person he meets adores him. However. He's very annoying to Llama. He keeps trying to steal her food and play with her, but since she's older, she doesn't really have that sense of whimsy and jolliness that he does.

Fun fact: the Beatles wrote their song "Octopuss Garden" about my cat (real fact). Therefore that is the number one Beatle song of all time. Octopuss does indeed have a garden (kind of). He shares one with my older sister who doesn't really want him in the garden. However. He is in there.
